The warm and sociable environment of the hotel roof garden sitting provides a fitting setting for the tasting of the La Palma world-renowned wines. At the start of the night the guests will be served snacks on the side, all crafted with utmost care and attention to detail, while using locally sourced ingredients, to accompany the special beverages.

A Stellar Experience

Guests will be able to taste a variety of wines, and order from an intricate variety of local delicacies. Guests will also be able to partake in various artistic activities such as hand painting, clay modelling and more!
